Who are we?

We are a team that believes in the goodness of home cooked food. Our mission is to promote home cooked food consumption among people by empowering home cooked food delivering start-ups. We are accomplishing our mission by connecting hungry stomachs with nearby amazing home cooked food vendors via the Home Chef common platform.
